Attracted by its pristine beauty and the campus's emphasis on technical excellence, students are drawn from just around the corner as well as from far across the globe. Eminent academicians and corporate professionals share their knowledge and experiences with the students and help them apply their theoretical knowledge to real-life projects. Academic counselors mentor the students, acting as their friend, philosopher and guide.

All Schools and Research Centers on campus are committed to generating, disseminating and preserving knowledge. They seek to develop in each member of the campus, the ability and passion to work wisely, creatively and effectively for the betterment of humanity. Numerous seminars, discussions and debates help enhance the quest for knowledge.

The campus is located in the picturesque village of Ettimadai, Coimbatore in Tamil Nadu. The campus is spread across 400 acres of land. The campus is well connected by road, rail and air. The nearest airport is Coimbatore International Airport, which is about 27 km from the campus. The nearest railway station is Coimbatore Junction, which is about 24 km from the campus. The campus is about 22 km from the Gandhipuram Central Bus Stand.

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham has been accredited with the highest grade of 'A' by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) for five years, and has been ranked as one of the best research universities in India. The Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India, has ranked Amrita as the 8th best university in India in the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) Ranking 2020. The university has also been ranked 4th in the Swachh Campus Rankings 2019, 9th in the Atal Ranking of Institutions on Innovation Achievements (ARIIA) 2020, and 13th in the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) Ranking 2019 for Medical colleges in India.
